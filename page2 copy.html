NOLI ME TANGERE

CHARACTER DESCRIPTIONS
Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin (Ibarra)
  • Ibarra is a young, wealthy mestizo who has recently returned to the Philippines from seven years of studying in Europe
  • He is smart, well-respected, and very idealistic.
Maria Clara
  • She is a woman of high social status and is believed to be Father Dámaso's goddaughter as well as daughter of Capitan Tiago
  • She is truly Father Dámaso's biogical child, the result of their scandalous relationship with Capitan Tiago's wife.
Padre Dámaso
  • Father Dámaso is an elderly, ruthlessly corrupt, and Spanish priest who has spent almost two decades residing among the native Filipinos.
  • He has spent all that time among them, but the years have done nothing to endear him or give him any sympathy for his "flock."
  • He is profoundly prejudiced, spiteful, and vengeful, and he has no problem using his immense influence to destroy people who have wronged him.
Kapitan Tiago
  • The "father" of Maria Clara and a business man who was very wealthy and influential in San Diego
  • He wanted to be positioned amongst the country's elites and valued more on social connections than anything else.
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • He is the father of Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra and the friend of Lieutenant Guevara.
  • He witnessed a tax collector beating up a child and to stop him, Don Rafael accidently killed the tax collector and was imprisoned.
  • His enemies chose to slander him but when he was supposed to be free, he died in the cell.
Padre Salvi
  • He was the curate of San Diego and had greatly influenced the people living there.
  • Padre Salvi's enemy was Alperes because they both wanted power of the town and they tried to annoy each other.
  • He was corrupt and lusted on Maria Clara who also spied on her while bathing.
Alperes
  • A man who is irritated and unhappy with his current marriage with Donya Consolacion
  • He would constantly slander on Padre Salvi to get on his nerves and irritate him.
Tiya Isabel
  • The caretaker of Maria Clara and Tiago's cousin
Donya Victorina de Espadaña
  • She was a friend of Kapitan Tiago and the wife of Don Tiburcio
  • Wanted to only marry a Spaniard to maintain her social status and even tried to act to be a Spanish woman.
Donya Consolacion
  • An old Filipina who is married with the ensign and she would constantly have intense fights with him.
  • She is very vulgar and violent and even is rivals with Padre Salvi as well as pretending to not know Tagalog and instead spoke broken spanish.
Elias
  • He's a fugitive and wants to improve in society because Crisostomo Ibarra saved his life.
  • In his youth, he was arrogant because of his wealth but lost everything and became a selfless man.
  • He worked with Captain Pablo's bandits and dedicated to helping the oppressed.
Don Tiburcio de Epadaña
  • A Spaniard married to Donya Victorina and he was brought to the Philippines as a customs officer but was dismissed.
  • He's thirty-five years old despite looking older and instead practiced medicine when having not having not much money despite him not being a doctor.
Pilosopo Tasyo o Don Anastacio
  • He is very smart and loved learning and philosophy since he was a student, he became an old scholar in San Diego
  • He bought too many books throughout his life that he became poor
  • He served as an adviser for the townspeople in San Diego during his last few years of his life.
Sisa
  • She was a mother who was abused by her husband and her two sons were Basilio and Crispin
  • When her sons both went missing, she was arrested by the Guardia Civil and was humiliated to the point of insanity.
Crispin
  • He is a young smart boy and a sacristan of their church
  • The head sacristan and Padre Salvi gave him a punishment and was beaten up by the sacristan mayor because of being accused of the stealing of two pieces of gold.
  • Padre Salvi and the sacristan mayor was assumed to be the ones who kill him.
Basilio
  • He is the older brother of Crispin and had also became a sacristan
  • Basilio fled when the sacristan mayor started beating them, he encountered his mother after two months and she had gone insane and died with moments of lucidity before her death
  • He had cremated Elias and Sisa, Kapitan Tiago took him in and he became a medical student in Manila.
